About me

Hi, I'm Gonçalo Szabo Rio, a Junior Web Developer from Porto.

From an early age, I was passionate about gaming and technology, which led me to create two media download websites at a time when streaming services didn’t exist. This experience sparked my interest in IT and web development.

Academically, I pursued a degree in Physical Education and Sports, during which I attended the Erasmus program for one year in Brașov, Romania. I then completed a Master’s in Teaching, gaining valuable experience as a schoolteacher.

Later, I co-founded Minuto90, a sports website where I worked as a journalist and database manager, covering football games and managing player statistics.

Afterwards, I spent seven years in the Portuguese Army, graduating from both the Sergeants' and Officers' School. During my service, I attended courses such as Cisco's IT Essentials and Microsoft's Management and Security of Local Networks, which further deepened my interest in IT.

After leaving the army, I decided to pursue my passion for technology and enrolled in the Pro Mov Reskilling 4 Employment program at Cesae Digital. I’m currently pursuing a Software Developer course, determined to turn my early interests into a successful professional career.
